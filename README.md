# Tobacco Use Trends in the United States

## 📊 Overview
This project analyzes the **prevalence and trends in tobacco use across the United States**, drawing from the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS). By breaking down data by state and region, the project explores how smoking habits have changed over time and identifies high-risk areas for better health outcomes.

Smoking is a major contributor to premature deaths and health complications. Understanding historic usage rates helps guide strategies to reduce these negative impacts.

---

## 📂 Dataset Information
The dataset includes:
- **State**: US state for each record
- **Smoke Everyday**: % of people who smoke daily
- **Smoke Some Days**: % of people who smoke occasionally
- **Former Smoker**: % of people who previously smoked
- **Never Smoked**: % of people who have never smoked
- **Location 1**: Geographic region (Census divisions or regions)
